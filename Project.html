About

Dev Growth club is a tech club of Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University started by Adtya Kanodia. The club is among one of the most active clubs in the university. The club has been continously oragnising workshops, sessions, and bootcamps on diffrent topics realted to tech. The sessions were conducted on Linux, Github, Java, Blockchain, Web Devlopement etc.
